15.60.2.2  ERRORS
   - Storage  is  allocated for DX,DY,DZ.  You must enter
the errors following the values for the dependent variable.  The errors on
the independent variables are equal to the bin width.

                                  example
READ MESH WITH ERRORS
FOR X=1,2,3
FOR Y=5
    Z=5,.5,6,.2,7,.1
FOR Y=8
    Z=9,.1,8,.1,7,.1
