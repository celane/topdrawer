15.63.3  HISTOGRAMS
 

SAVE HISTOGRAMS
     [FILE=file_name]
     [IDENT=[FROM] [n] [TO n]]
     [ENTRIES[=ON|OFF]]
     [HISTOGRAM[=ON|OFF]]
     [RELATIVE=[=ON|OFF]] [SHARE=[=ON|OFF]] [RECLENGTH[=n]]
     [SELECT|NAME='hist_name']
     [{WRITE|STORE}]
     [UPDATE[=ON|OFF]]
     [AREA|DIRECTORY[="directory"]]
     [TReE[=ON|OFF]]
Saves  the  foreign  histograms.   Some  options  apply  only  to specific
histogram packages.
